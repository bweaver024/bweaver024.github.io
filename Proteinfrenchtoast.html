Protein French Toast

French toast is usually seen as a comfort food, but with a few modifications we can dial back those calories.

PROTEIN FRENCH TOAST RECIPE

Ingredients:

Your bread of choice (under 100 calories per slice)
60 grams of egg whites per slice (adjust to your liking)
2 drops of liquid stevia per slice or one packet of slevia per slice
As much or as little of cinnamon as you like
Fruit of your choice (I usually use blueberries)
Sugar-Free Syrup (Mrs. Butterworth is my go to) I buy this from amazon

Steps:
  1. 1. Preheat nonstick pan on medium heat.


  2. 2. Mix egg whites, stevia and cinnamon in a baking dish (I just use left over take out containers).


  3. 3. Dip the slices of bread in the egg white solution, making sure saturate each side. (note if using white bread it will absorb quickly and the bread will become very delicate).


  4. 4. Spray pan with cooking spray and lay down your french toast.


  5. 5. It's better to cook these a little slower to ensure the center is fully cooked. Should take 5-8 minutes.


  6. 6. Once french toast is fully cooked add your favorite sugar-free syrup


  7. 7. Optional: add fruits ontop of your french toast. Sometimes I like to add the blueberries to the pan before putting my french toast down.
